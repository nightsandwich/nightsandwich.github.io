Life in Box City

Conway's Game of Life in action

Click a cell to make it alive/dead. DON'T CARE will pick for you.

ITERATE ONCE will display one cycle of evolution.

AUTO-PLAY will display Box City's full evolution ending in death or immortality!

PAUSE will stop AUTO-PLAY.
Don't forget to RESET the board before each game.
